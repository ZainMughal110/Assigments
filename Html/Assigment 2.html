Recipe of Biryani


Ingredients


1.Rice
  1. 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)

  2. 4 cups water

  3. 2-3 cloves

  4. 2-3 green cardamoms

  5. 1 bay leaf

  6. 1-inch cinnamon stick

  7. 1 tsp salt

2.For Chicken Marinade
  1. 500g chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces)

  2. ½ cup yogurt

  3. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

  4. 1 tsp red chili powder

  5. ½ tsp turmeric powder

  6. 1 tsp garam masala

  7. ½ tsp cumin powder

  8. ½ tsp coriander powder

  9. 1 tsp salt

  10. 1 tbsp lemon juice

3.For Biryani Masala
  1. 2 tbsp oil or ghee

  2. 1 large onion (sliced)

  3. 1 large tomato (chopped)

  4. 1 green chili (chopped)

  5. ½ tsp cumin seeds

  6. 1 tsp biryani masala

  7. 1 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

  8. 1 tbsp fresh mint leaves (chopped)

4.For Assembling
  1. 1 pinch saffron (soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk) or ½ tsp yellow food color

  2. 2 tbsp fried onions

  3. 1 tbsp ghee
